CadenceKey

This is CadenceKey, a tool to find your vocal range based on your lowest and highest notes. Press the enable recording button to allow your device microphone to listen. First sing your low note, and once it is stable, press the record low note button. Repeat for the high note and your result will be shown onscreen. If you wish to repeat or start again, disable and enable the recording once more.
